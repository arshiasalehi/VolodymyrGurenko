About Me

Professional Tennis Player

I am a dedicated and competitive tennis player with a strong foundation in both national and international circuits. Born in Ukraine, I began my tennis journey at the age of three and quickly ascended to rank among the top five juniors nationally.

Currently residing in Canada, I hold the #4 national ranking in U18 singles and #3 in U18 doubles. On the international stage, I have achieved an ITF Junior ranking of 130.

Recognized as an Elite Tennis Player in Quebec, I am committed to continuous improvement and aspire to transition into the professional circuit.
